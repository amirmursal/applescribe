Business Relationship is a key constituent of any successful venture. We at Medinetcorpusa completely understand and embrace it by maintaining mutually rewarding relationship with customers, partners, employees, vendors & stakeholders.

Medinetcorpusa has developed several partners across the globe from spectrums like healthcare facility, physician group, individual physicians, sales & marketing organizations, software development firms, web designing companies, small sized entrepreneurs and business-minded individuals. We have muster out classified partnership programs for all.

Based on the business dynamics, Medinetcorpusa offers 3 different partnership programs which caters to profiles of various types of partners. These Partnership Programs enable you grow your business, expand your service domain & increase its profitability, regardless of its current size and scale. It also helps you add new revenue streams to your existing business by offering broader range of services. For some enterprising individuals, it has offered an opportunity to start new business/revenue model without a need for domain knowledge and technical know how.

As a partner, you are empowered with adequate sales support resources to expediate your sales cycle. Our cost-effective and proficient business solutions not only enhance your delivery capacity but also make you more competitive in your local market.

MY BRAND PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM

My Brand Partnership Program is designed for established companies who already have the ability to market services to a large number of clients & who wish to increase their delivery capabilities without increasing costs. This Partnership program allows your company to offer all of Medinetcorpusa's services under your own name minus the headache of operations and deliverance.

Consider Medinetcorpusa as your extended operational arm. You sell our services on your own terms & pricing structure to the end client. Focus your ability and efforts on selling the services while we efficiently manage and deliver the assignment/project or services. Medinetcorpusa remains completely anonymous to your clients.

FRANCHISE PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM

Franchise Partnership Program is devised for individuals & small companies who have sales expertise, domain knowledge, social network but lack established market or right brand/services to project for sales conversion. It is also meant for companies/individuals who have market access but lack domain knowledge and want to explore new stream of revenue or business model.

This program provides you with a readymade & reputed brand in healthcare, technology and business process outsourcing which can be merchandised further for expeditious and consistent earnings. You get to market and sell array of our services by showcasing impressive track record, portfolios, references & testimonials to the sales leads. This enables you to speed up sales cycle and increase conversion rates. You are our front-face and marketing arm in your local area with complete assistance from us in sales and support department.

We also offer you flexibility of buying your own local domain and hosting. Using frame forwarding technology, Medinetcorpusa will encompass its service gamut on your domain.

REFERRAL PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM

In the era of globalization, individuals or small/medium sized entrepreneurs who have access to a network with large community of prospect can generate revenue for themselves even from the industry they have little or no knowledge.

Over the years, Medinetcorpusa has actuated this opportunity to the collective benefit of company and its referral partners. You do not need to have any domain knowledge in healthcare, technology or business process outsourcing for referring prospects. You are not required to involve in any hassles of sales cycle, process transition, technical knowhow, project or client management. You can either earn a project based one time commission or a continuous stream of revenue for businesses like medical billing, medical transcription, data processing and non-medical transcription. For these businesses, each referral that turns into our client makes you earn a lifetime of income like a royalty income. You receive your paycheck as long as the client you referred enjoys our services.

Facilities referred by you would never know about your referral commissions but would be grateful to you for referring them to a reliable, efficient and cost-effective service provider.
