About us

Great sermons can't afford to be forgotten. Not for you, not for your congregation!!!

Church transcription refers to transcribing all Church recorded material into text copies, making it permanently available for wider audiences and future reference/discourse. Medinetcorp is honored in offering helping hand in spreading the name of God. Record your sermons, lectures, prayers etc.. with our available digital dictation facility or send us your existing recordings through internet/DVD. We not only have the ability to transcribe but translate it in many languages. Can arrange to post them on your website and broaden your audience.

Trust us for accuracy and affordability of the transcription. We have a team of qualified transcribers with proficiency in biblical sayings and religions scriptures.

Why text transcripts of Church sermons are important?

  • Maximize your reach to a larger audience across borders and faiths.
  • Transcripts are available anywhere and anytime therefore can be used as reference point for future discourses.
  • Transcripts can help in making leaflet or newsletter which can be distributed to the congregation.
  • Transcripts of relgious discourses are treasure and can be used as a souvenir for patrons of the church.
  • Great transcripts enables you to attract sponsors and donors.
